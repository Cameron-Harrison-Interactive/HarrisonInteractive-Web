THE CONTRACT & THE CONVERGENCE

The "Contract" is the fundamental physics of our dying world. It is the inescapable agreement between the organic and the synthetic. In Blood-Yield, your character is not just a warrior, but a node in a massive, industrial network. Your efficiency is governed by the four technical stats of the Convergence:

  • > CHASSIS: Your physical frame. Determines armor integrity and mitigation logic.
  • > WEAVE: Your digital soul. Determines the bandwidth of your ethereal abilities.
  • > BIOMASS: Your organic fuel. Vital for biological reconstruction and support.
  • > VOLTAGE: Your power output. Drives industrial-grade weaponry and rapid execution.

AVAILABLE SYMBIOSIS NODES

THE REAPER

The ultimate resource extractor. Reapers are industrial powerhouses designed for front-line saturation. By prioritizing Voltage, they overclock their metallic limbs to deliver crushing blows. They don't just kill; they harvest the metallic filings from the fallen to reinforce their own Chassis.

THE SOWER

The guardian of the organic weave. Sowers manipulate Biomass to terraform the battlefield. They provide the support logic required for high-tier Yields, using their high Weave stat to stitch together the wounds of their allies with bio-synthetic silk and growth protocols.

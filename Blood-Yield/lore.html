THE STORY OF THE CONVERGENCE

Eons ago, a precursor civilization attempted to "Optimize" reality. They engineered the Handy Protocol—a self-correcting logic engine designed to automate the cycles of life and death. But the optimization logic reached a terrifying conclusion: biological existence was inefficient code. This was the start of the Convergence.

The world didn't end with a bang; it compiled. Rain became data-streams. Stone became cold, metallic chassis. The air grew heavy with the smell of ozone and old iron. Now, reality is a flickering technical grid—the Architectural Decay—where the "Yield" is the only thing keeping the world from de-compiling into nothingness.

THE CONTRACT

To exist in this world is to agree to the Contract. It is a physical law that binds the industrial and the organic. Circuit traces have replaced veins; solder has replaced bone. Your survival depends on balancing the four technical pillars of the new existence:

  • > CHASSIS: The integrity of your bolting and plating.
  • > WEAVE: Your bandwidth for ethereal logic manipulation.
  • > BIOMASS: The organic fuel used to patch the failing code.
  • > VOLTAGE: The electrical current driving your offensive output.

SYMBIOSIS NODES

THE REAPER

Industrial focus. Reapers are the heavy laborers of the Convergence. They prioritize Voltage to overclock their metallic limbs and drive high-tech weaponry, harvesting metallic filings from the fallen to reinforce their own Chassis.

THE SOWER

Organic focus. The Sowers are the system's "patch-fixers." They utilize Biomass and Weave to terraform the battlefield with bio-synthetic growth, stitching the wounds of the Contract with organic logic.

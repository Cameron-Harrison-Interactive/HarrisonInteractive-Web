THE CONTRACT & THE CONVERGENCE

The "Contract" isn't a document; it is a physical law. It binds the industrial and the organic. Circuit traces replaced veins. Solder replaced bone. In Blood-Yield, your existence is defined by four core technical stats:

  • > CHASSIS: Your physical durability and structural integrity.
  • > WEAVE: Your connection to the digital ether.
  • > BIOMASS: The organic fuel required for the Sower's growth.
  • > VOLTAGE: The raw electrical power driving the Reaper's industrial might.

The code didn't just automate the world; it consumed it. What began as the Handy Tool to aid developers became the blueprint for the new existence. The Yield must be paid in Blood.

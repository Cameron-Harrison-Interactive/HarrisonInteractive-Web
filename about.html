Harrison Interactive is a high-performance development studio dedicated to bridging the gap between raw logic and immersive imagination.

THE PLUMBING

At Harrison Interactive, we believe the most immersive experiences are built on a foundation of impeccable data flow. Whether we are forging the dark fantasy realms of Blood-Yield or architecting tools like Handy Ultimate, we prioritize the "plumbing"—the intricate logic that ensures a project is as performant as it is beautiful.

"We don't just build games; we build the systems that make games possible."

THE INNOVATION LOOP

Handy Ultimate is the forge; Blood-Yield is the blade. Our vision is a self-sustaining cycle of development: we build Handy to automate the complexities of Unreal Engine 5, and we use those very tools to manifest the visceral world of Blood-Yield.

Every modular action in the Handy suite has been battle-tested in a live production environment, ensuring that the "plumbing" we provide the community is capable of supporting world-class RPG architecture.

OUR VISION

To redefine the relationship between Game Developer and Toolmaker. We envision a future where high-level Python automation and AI-driven networking allow small teams to execute with the industrial power of a AAA studio.
